### Gregor’s presentation

- Gregor’s Conversation Starter on the Fair Share Cost Token idea: “Imagine a 'vulnerability contact tracing' system \-- combining a corporate early warning system with privacy-preserving payment processing in exchange for sustaining open source security teams. This doesn't need to be a pipe-dream, we already have the technology. We invented and operated it during COVID in Germany. That is why we should develop a similar tool for F/OSS."  
  - This topic is a follow-up to Gregor’s FOSS BackStage presentation on the FSCT.  
  - Recording is not available yet, but will be shared when it is.
       
- Æva: IMHO, it is less about some pre-disclosure notification and simply about finding a way for Manufacturers to engage in the information sharing \*without\* needing to publish (even indirectly, by observing what notifications they subscribe to) their SBOM and such a notification process is orthogonal to payment for voluntary security attestations
